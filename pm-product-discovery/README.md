# pm-product-discovery

Continuous product discovery skills from opportunity mapping to experiment design.

## Skills (5)

- **continuous-discovery** — Run continuous discovery cycles with Opportunity Solution Trees.
- **user-interview** — Design interview scripts and synthesize notes into JTBD statements and opportunities.
- **prioritization** — Score and rank backlogs using RICE, ICE, MoSCoW, Kano, or Opportunity Scoring.
- **assumption-testing** — Map riskiest assumptions and design cheap validation experiments.
- **feature-request-analysis** — Analyze batches of customer requests into themes and strategic opportunities.

## Commands (5)

- `/pm-product-discovery:discover` — Run a full discovery cycle from opportunity mapping to experiment design.
- `/pm-product-discovery:brainstorm` — Generate and evaluate solution ideas for a specific opportunity.
- `/pm-product-discovery:interview` — Prepare an interview script or synthesize interview notes.
- `/pm-product-discovery:prioritize` — Score and rank a backlog with a structured framework.
- `/pm-product-discovery:triage-requests` — Analyze customer feature requests into themes and priorities.
