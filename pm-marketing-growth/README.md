# pm-marketing-growth

Product marketing and growth skills for sustainable user acquisition and retention.

## Skills (3)

- **positioning** — Define product positioning using the competitive alternatives framework.
- **north-star-metric** — Define a North Star Metric with business game classification and input metrics.
- **growth-loops** — Design growth loops and identify the highest-leverage growth opportunities.

## Commands (3)

- `/pm-marketing-growth:market-product` — Develop positioning, messaging, and marketing strategy.
- `/pm-marketing-growth:north-star` — Define your North Star Metric and supporting input metrics.
- `/pm-marketing-growth:growth-strategy` — Design growth loops and identify growth levers.
