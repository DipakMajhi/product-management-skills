# pm-toolkit

PM career and professional utilities for every stage of the product management journey.

## Skills (7)

- **review-resume** — Review and score a PM resume across 8 best-practice dimensions with specific before/after rewrites.
- **tailor-resume** — Align a PM resume to a specific job description with keyword gap analysis.
- **grammar-check** — Proofread PM writing for grammar, clarity, logic, and professional tone.
- **pm-career-ladder** — Assess a PM's current career level and build a 90-day promotion plan.
- **interview-prep-coach** — Coach PM interview answers across behavioral, product design, strategy, metrics, and estimation question types.
- **draft-nda** — Draft an NDA template for vendor evaluations, contractor engagements, co-founder discussions, or advisor relationships.
- **privacy-policy** — Draft a privacy policy covering GDPR and CCPA compliance basics.

## Commands (7)

- `/pm-toolkit:review-resume` — Score and review a PM resume with specific improvement suggestions.
- `/pm-toolkit:tailor-resume` — Tailor a PM resume to a specific job description.
- `/pm-toolkit:career-growth` — Assess your PM career level and build a 90-day promotion plan.
- `/pm-toolkit:career-check` — Quick check of where you stand on the PM career ladder.
- `/pm-toolkit:interview-prep` — Prepare for a PM interview with frameworks and answer critique.
- `/pm-toolkit:proofread` — Polish any PM document for grammar, clarity, and impact.
- `/pm-toolkit:draft-nda` — Draft an NDA for common PM and startup contexts.
