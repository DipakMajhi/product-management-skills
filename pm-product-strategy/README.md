# pm-product-strategy

Product strategy skills covering vision, competitive positioning, business models, pricing, and market sizing.

## Skills (8)

- **product-strategy-canvas** — Build a comprehensive product strategy using the 9-section canvas covering vision, segments, value propositions, trade-offs, metrics, growth, capabilities, and defensibility.
- **product-vision** — Craft an inspiring, specific, and emotionally resonant product vision in three formats.
- **value-proposition** — Design a value proposition using the 6-part Jobs-to-Be-Done framework.
- **competitive-analysis** — Map the competitive landscape and derive strategic differentiation opportunities.
- **business-model** — Design or evaluate a business model using Business Model Canvas or Lean Canvas.
- **pricing-strategy** — Design a pricing strategy with tier structure and willingness-to-pay analysis.
- **market-sizing** — Estimate TAM, SAM, and SOM using top-down and bottom-up approaches.
- **strategic-frameworks** — Apply SWOT, PESTLE, Porter's Five Forces, and Ansoff Matrix.

## Commands (6)

- `/pm-product-strategy:strategy` — Build a complete 9-section product strategy canvas.
- `/pm-product-strategy:vision` — Craft or sharpen a product vision statement.
- `/pm-product-strategy:value-proposition` — Design a value proposition using the JTBD framework.
- `/pm-product-strategy:competitive-scan` — Map and analyze the competitive landscape.
- `/pm-product-strategy:business-model` — Design or evaluate a business model.
- `/pm-product-strategy:pricing` — Design a pricing strategy with tiers and validation plan.
- `/pm-product-strategy:market-size` — Calculate TAM, SAM, and SOM for your market.
