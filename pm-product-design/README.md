# pm-product-design

Design skills for product managers to work effectively with design teams and evaluate UX.

## Skills (3)

- **design-thinking** — Facilitate design thinking sessions using the Double Diamond methodology and HMW statements.
- **ux-critique** — Evaluate product UX against established usability heuristics with severity scoring.
- **design-brief** — Write a design brief that gives designers the context and constraints to produce the right solution.

## Commands (3)

- `/pm-product-design:design-sprint` — Plan or facilitate a design thinking session or design sprint.
- `/pm-product-design:critique-design` — Evaluate a product or feature design against usability principles.
- `/pm-product-design:write-brief` — Write a design brief for a designer to work from.
