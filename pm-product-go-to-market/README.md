# pm-product-go-to-market

Go-to-market strategy skills for product launches and market entry.

## Skills (2)

- **gtm-strategy** — Build a complete GTM strategy with beachhead segment, GTM motion, and launch plan.
- **icp-builder** — Define an Ideal Customer Profile with firmographics, personas, and trigger events.

## Commands (3)

- `/pm-product-go-to-market:plan-launch` — Build a complete go-to-market plan for a product or feature launch.
- `/pm-product-go-to-market:define-icp` — Define your Ideal Customer Profile.
- `/pm-product-go-to-market:battlecard` — Create a competitive battlecard for sales and positioning.
