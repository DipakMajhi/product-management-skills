# Product Roadmap Builder

A roadmap is a communication tool. Its job is to tell a coherent story about where the product is going and why, not to be a detailed project plan.

## Roadmap Types

Apply this skill to: $ARGUMENTS


### Now / Next / Later
- **Now**: What is in active development this quarter
- **Next**: What is planned for the next 1-2 quarters (committed or near-committed)
- **Later**: What is on the horizon (directional, not committed)

Best for: Teams that want to reduce over-promising without losing stakeholder visibility.

### Outcome-Based Roadmap
Instead of listing features by time, group work by the outcome it is driving:
- Outcome: Reduce onboarding drop-off from 65% to 45%
  - Initiative: Redesign step 2 of onboarding
  - Initiative: Add progress indicator
  - Initiative: In-app guided tour

Best for: Teams that have adopted OKRs and want to connect features to goals.

### Theme-Based Roadmap
Group work by strategic theme across time horizons:
- Theme: "Make it fast for power users"
- Theme: "Unlock enterprise buyers"
- Theme: "Expand into mobile"

Best for: Leadership presentations and investor updates.

## Transforming Feature Lists into Outcome Roadmaps

When given a feature backlog:
1. For each feature, ask: what outcome does this drive? (retention, acquisition, activation, revenue, NPS)
2. Group features by shared outcome
3. Sequence outcome groups by strategic priority and dependency
4. Within each outcome group, sequence features by dependency and impact

## Roadmap Communication Anti-Patterns

- Committing to specific dates for items more than 6 weeks out
- Listing features without explaining the problem they solve
- Having more than 3-4 themes on a single roadmap slide
- Using "Q3" dates without a year
- Presenting to different audiences with the same level of detail

## Output Template

---
**PRODUCT ROADMAP: [Product Name]**
**Format:** [Now/Next/Later | Outcome-Based | Theme-Based]
**Period Covered:** [Timeframe]

**Strategic Context**
[1-2 sentences on what this roadmap is optimizing for in this period]

**Now (Current Quarter)**
| Item | Type | Outcome | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Feature/Initiative] | [Feature/Fix/Debt] | [Outcome it drives] | [In progress/Planned] |

**Next (Following Quarter)**
[Same structure, note these are directional]

**Later (Future)**
[High-level themes or initiatives, no commitments]

**What Is NOT on This Roadmap**
[2-3 notable items that were deprioritized and why - this builds trust by showing trade-offs were considered]

**Dependencies and Risks**
[Key items that could shift this roadmap]
