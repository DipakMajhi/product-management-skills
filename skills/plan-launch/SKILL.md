# Plan Launch

This skill routes to **gtm-strategy**, which provides a complete go-to-market strategy framework from beachhead selection through launch execution and expansion.

**When to use this:**
- Launching a new product or a major feature to market
- Defining the GTM motion: should this be PLG, sales-led, or a hybrid?
- Selecting a beachhead segment to win first before expanding
- Building a launch checklist: pre-launch, launch week, post-launch
- Answering "how would you take this product to market?" in PM or founder interviews

**Quick GTM motion guide by ACV:**
- < $1K/year → Product-led growth (self-serve): frictionless trial, low TTV, in-product upgrade
- $1K - $5K/year → PLG + inside sales hybrid: self-serve trial, sales assists expansion
- $5K - $50K/year → Inside sales + PLG: sales-led pipeline, product as proof
- $50K - $100K/year → Field sales: consultative cycle, CS-heavy, multi-stakeholder
- > $100K/year → Enterprise + ABM: named accounts, procurement, long cycle, executive sponsors

**What gtm-strategy covers:**
- Beachhead segment selection: Geoffrey Moore Crossing the Chasm + Bowling Pin expansion strategy
- ICP definition for the launch segment: firmographics, buyer/champion/user roles, trigger events
- GTM motion selection matrix: ACV, complexity, buyer behavior, product-channel fit
- Land-and-expand strategy: how to sequence growth from beachhead to adjacent segments
- Launch phasing: alpha (design partners) → beta (early access) → general availability (GA)
- Channel-market fit analysis: which acquisition channels fit the ICP's buying behavior
- Pre-launch checklist: messaging, positioning, sales enablement, press/analyst outreach, partner activation
- Launch week playbook: day-by-day actions for product, marketing, sales, and CS
- Post-launch: 30/60/90-day review plan, success metrics, expansion triggers
- Launch anti-patterns: launching to everyone, no beachhead focus, skipping the beta phase

**The skill will:**
1. Ask about the product, target customer, and stage of launch
2. Select the GTM motion based on ACV and buyer behavior
3. Define the beachhead segment with selection rationale
4. Build the Bowling Pin sequence for post-launch expansion
5. Design the launch phasing: alpha → beta → GA
6. Deliver the launch checklist by function (product, marketing, sales, CS)
7. Define leading and lagging success metrics for the launch
